Content Writer Intern

Job Title: Content Writer Intern

Company: ParvasPages.in

Location: Pune, Maharashtra

Job Type: Internship

Job Summary:

ParvasPages.in is looking for a creative and talented Content Writer Intern to assist in producing high-quality content for our website, blog, and social media channels. This internship is ideal for freshers or final-year students of any qualified university or individuals with an equivalent diploma.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in researching and writing engaging and informative content for our website, blog, and social media
  • Help develop content strategies that align with our brand and business goals
  • Proofread and edit content for accuracy and clarity
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to create content that drives traffic and engagement
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices
  • Ensure all content is consistent with our brand voice and style

Qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a degree/diploma in English, Journalism, Marketing, or related field
  • Excellent writing, editing, and proofreading skills
  • Strong research skills
  • Ability to write in a variety of styles and tones
  • Basic understanding of SEO and content marketing principles
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines
  • Good communication and collaboration skills
