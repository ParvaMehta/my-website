Accountant Intern

Job Title: Accountant Intern

Company: ParvasPages.in

Location: Pune, Maharashtra

Job Type: Internship

Job Summary:

ParvasPages.in is seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Accountant Intern to assist in managing financial transactions and preparing financial reports. This internship is ideal for freshers or final-year students of any qualified university or individuals with an equivalent diploma.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in maintaining accurate and up-to-date financial records
  • Help prepare and analyze financial statements and reports
  • Support the management of accounts payable and receivable
  • Assist in reconciling bank statements
  • Ensure compliance with financial regulations and tax laws
  • Support budgeting and forecasting activities
  • Assist in conducting internal audits
  • Provide financial insights and recommendations to management

Qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a degree/diploma in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles
  • Proficiency in MS Office, especially Excel
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
